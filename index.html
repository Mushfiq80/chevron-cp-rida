About Chevron

Chevron Corporation, a global leader in the energy sector, has a significant presence in Bangladesh, focusing on oil and gas exploration, production, refining, and marketing. Established as the Pacific Coast Oil Company in 1879, Chevron expanded its operations globally, including in Bangladesh in the mid-1990s after acquiring Unocal. The company operates three major gas fields in Bangladesh, collectively supplying over 60% of the country's natural gas demand and 80% of its condensate production. Chevron's mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and cleaner energy to improve people's lives and meet the world's growing energy needs. The company operates with a decentralized organizational structure, divided into upstream and downstream segments. In Bangladesh, Chevron's operations are managed by a local subsidiary, which collaborates closely with government agencies and other stakeholders.
